Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is AI Interview Trainer?

AI Interview Trainer is a smart platform that helps users practice interviews using AI-generated questions, automatic scoring, and instant feedback to improve performance.

🎤 How do I start an interview?

Go to the Interview section from the sidebar, click on Start Interview, and answer the questions presented by the AI system.

🌍 What languages are supported?

The platform supports English, Bhojpuri, and Maithili languages.

⭐ How is my performance scored?

Your performance is scored by AI based on answer quality, clarity, relevance, and communication skills.

📊 How is the score calculated?

Scores are generated automatically based on your answers, communication clarity, and relevance using AI evaluation models.

📁 Where can I see past interview records?

All completed interviews are stored in the Records section. You can review answers, scores, and feedback anytime.
